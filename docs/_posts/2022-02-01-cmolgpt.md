## cMolGPT - A Conditional Generative Pre-Trained Transformer for Molecular Design


---

**cMolGPT** is a *conditional generative pre-trained Transformer* for **target-aware molecular generation**.  
By conditioning on protein targets, cMolGPT generates chemically valid, diverse, and biologically relevant molecules tailored to specific therapeutic contexts.

**Key features:**

- Target-specific de novo molecule generation  
- High chemical validity and structural diversity  
- Improved hit discovery efficiency  
- Generalizable across multiple drug targets
